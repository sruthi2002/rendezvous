ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

Dating back to the early 21st century, B.E Computer Science and Engineering programme has a long history of excellence. It is designed to be a creative driving force at R.M.D Engineering college and worldwide, of highest scholarly and entrepreneurial quality.The department also focuses on giving hands-on industrial experience to the students by organizing various Center Of Excellence (COE), where the students get trained by the professionals from various firms right from third semster up until they get recruited by those firms. The various COEs available are Big Data, Cyber Security, Cloud Computing and Digital Enterprise.





MISSION OF THE DEPARTMENT

MISSION:

Our mission is to inspire, educate, and produce computer engineers capable of tackling fundamental scientific problems and important societal challenges - and to do so with the highest commitment to quality, integrity, and respect for others. Our students are rigorously trained in fundamentals of engineering, with a strong bent towards the maker culture of learning and doing.

ABOUT Rendezvous 2K22

Rendezvous 2k22 will be a great platform to the students of various institutions to showcase their technical skills and their knowledge in the various new trends in the field of Computer Science. Special emphasis is laid to explore their talents so that their personality filters crystal clear, imbibing in themselves the virtues of integrity, discipline, confidence and dynamism and thus emerge successful in the modern competitive world.





Events

WINNERS OF ALL EVENTS CAN EXPERIENCE VR FOR FREE!!

Paper Presentation

BRING OUT THE IMPOSSIBLE

  1. Topics:
      (i) Li-Fi Technology
      (ii)Cloud Computing and Cloud Services
      (iii)Azure vs AWS
  2. NOTE: Other Technical topics are also welcomed. Pendrive, Hard disk are not allowed.
  3. Maximum of four authors per paper.
  4. Paper should be in IEEE format.
  5. Papers should only be in .doc format.
  6. The participants should bring their presentation on CD and two hardcopies of the paper.
  7. Each team will be allotted seven minutes for presentation and three minutes for queries.
  8. All papers must be sent to rmdsympossium22@gmail.com
  9. Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
Tech Hunt

THINK DIFFERENTLY

Each team is presented with a group of technical puzzles or pseudocodes or algorithms which teams must solve inorder to get final solution at end of hunt

  1. Maximum of four members per team.
Technical Quiz

BRING OUT THE GENIUS IN YOU

Technical Quiz brings out the hidden intellect of the students. Several questions on technical subjects will be posed to the students which helps to review their conceptual knowledge.

  1. An individual event consisting of 2 rounds.
  2. Technical terms should be decoded from the given set of clues in the photograph
BID AND DISCUSS

SHARE YOUR TECHNICAL THOUGHTS

The participant will be given with a technical topic to discuss and share their thoughts with participants.
